Arnis Assessment

Assessment Questions

Click on a question to reveal the answer:

  1. What is the national sport of the Philippines?
    2. Answer: Arnis
  2. What are the two main types of Arnis?
    3. Answer: Modern Arnis and Classical Arnis
  3. Name three basic stances used in Arnis.
    4. Answer: Forward stance, Back stance, Horse stance
  4. How many basic strikes are there in Arnis?
    5. Answer: 12 basic strikes
  5. What is the primary weapon used in Arnis?
    6. Answer: Rattan sticks
  6. What is the purpose of blocking in Arnis?
    7. Answer: To defend against an attack
  7. What is "Sinawali" in Arnis?
    8. Answer: A pattern-based drill that enhances coordination
  8. Why is footwork important in Arnis?
    9. Answer: It helps in mobility and positioning
  9. What is the difference between offensive and defensive techniques in Arnis?
    10. Answer: Offensive techniques focus on attacking, while defensive techniques focus on blocking and evading
  10. How does Arnis help in self-defense?
    11. Answer: It teaches weapon handling, blocking, and counter-attacks
