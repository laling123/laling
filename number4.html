NATIONAL MARTIAL ARTS AND SPORT IN THE PHILIPPINES: ARNIS

What is Arnis?

Arnis, also known as Eskrima or Kali, is the national martial art and sport of the Philippines. It is a weapon-based fighting system that primarily focuses on stick fighting but also includes techniques for bladed weapons, empty-hand combat, joint locks, grappling, and self-defense. Arnis has been practiced for centuries and was originally used by early Filipino warriors for self-defense and tribal warfare.

Main Goal of Arnis

Arnis is not just a sport but also an essential part of Filipino culture and history. It has influenced many forms of martial arts worldwide and is practiced globally. The training enhances physical fitness, discipline, and self-defense skills.

Many schools in the Philippines incorporate Arnis in their physical education curriculum, ensuring that younger generations appreciate and uphold this tradition.
